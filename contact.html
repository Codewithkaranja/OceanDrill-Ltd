Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and contact process

How quickly can you respond to an inquiry? +

We typically respond to inquiries within 2-4 hours during business hours. For urgent matters, please call our emergency line at +254 708 140 284 for immediate assistance.

Do you offer free site assessments? +

Yes, we offer free preliminary site assessments for drilling projects. Our technical team will visit your site to evaluate conditions and provide initial recommendations at no cost.

What areas in Kenya do you serve? +

We provide drilling services across Kenya, including Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, Eldoret, and surrounding regions. We also operate in select areas in India.

What information do I need to provide for a quote? +

For an accurate quote, please provide: project location, intended use (residential/commercial/agricultural), estimated water requirements, site access information, and any existing borehole details if applicable.

Do you assist with drilling permits and compliance? +

Yes, we provide comprehensive assistance with NEMA, WRA, and other regulatory requirements. We can guide you through the permitting process and ensure full compliance with all regulations.
