Our Story & Experience

Founded in the late 1990s, Ocean Drill Limited began as a small drilling operation with a single rig and a vision to provide reliable water access to communities across Kenya. Over the years, we have grown into a leading multidisciplinary drilling contractor with operations in both Kenya and India.

Our journey has been marked by continuous investment in modern equipment, training of skilled personnel, and expansion of our service offerings. Today, we operate a fleet of state-of-the-art drilling rigs capable of handling diverse geological conditions and project requirements.

What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to quality, safety, and environmental responsibility. Every project is approached with the same level of care and attention to detail, whether it's a residential borehole or a large-scale commercial drilling operation.

Our Mission

To deliver the highest quality drilling services at fair and transparent pricing while building strong client relationships based on mutual respect, professionalism, and trust.

  • Provide custom drilling solutions tailored to each client's unique needs
  • Continuously enhance our skills and upgrade our technology in line with industry standards
  • Maintain the highest standards of safety and environmental responsibility

Our Vision

To be recognized as one of the most trusted drilling service providers in Kenya — contributing to sustainable access to clean water and resilient infrastructure for communities now and in the future.

We envision a future where every community and business in Kenya has reliable access to clean water through sustainable drilling solutions that respect the environment and promote long-term water security.

Our vision extends beyond drilling services to becoming a comprehensive water solutions partner, involved in water conservation, management, and sustainable utilization initiatives across East Africa.
